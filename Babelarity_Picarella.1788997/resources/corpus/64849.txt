87 (eighty-seven) is the natural number following 86 and preceding 88.
87 is:
the sum of the squares of the first four primes.
the sum of the sums of the divisors of the first 10 positive integers.
the twenty-sixth distinct semiprime and the eighth of the form (3.q).
5! - 4! - 3! - 2! - 1! = 87
The aliquot sum of 87 is 33 within the aliquot sequence (87,33,15,9,4,3,1,0) 86 being the eighteenth composite number in the 3-aliquot tree.
Cricket in Australia holds 87 as a superstitiously unlucky score and is referred to as "the devil's number." This originates from the fact that 87 is 13 runs short of a century. 187, 287 and so on are also considered unlucky but is not as common as 87 on its own. As of this, some people will suffer from "Octokontaheptaphobia (Fear of 87)."
